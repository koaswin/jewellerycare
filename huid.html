HUID

Hallmark Unique Identification (HUID) number is a six-digit alphanumeric code consisting of numbers and letters. Every unit of jewellery will be given a HUID number during the hallmarking process, and it will be unique to that piece.

Hallmarking of gold has become mandatory since it verifies the precious metal's purity. At times, jewellers can sell gold jewellery claiming that it is 22 karat, even though it may actually be of lesser purity
